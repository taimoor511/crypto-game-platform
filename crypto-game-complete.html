📖 How to Play

Game Rules

Crypto Roulette is a 1v1 multiplayer game inspired by Buckshot Roulette. Two players compete using a revolver loaded with live and blank bullets.

Objective

  • Reduce opponent's health to 0 to win
  • Entry cost: 1 coin per match
  • Winner receives: 1.8 coins (0.2 platform fee)

Gameplay

  • Each round starts with 2-5 live bullets and 2-5 blank bullets (randomized)
  • Maximum 5 bullets per round
  • Coin flip determines who goes first
  • On your turn, choose to shoot opponent or shoot yourself (dodge)
  • Live bullet = 20 damage, Blank bullet = 0 damage
  • If you shoot yourself with a blank, you get another turn
  • After 5 turns or all bullets used, a new round starts with fresh bullets

Powers & Skills

Each player receives 3 random unique powers at the start of each round. Powers reset between rounds.

🚫 Skip Turn

Opponent misses their entire next turn. You get consecutive turns.

👁️ X-Ray

Reveals the current bullet type (live or blank) before you shoot.

💥 Double Damage

Your next shot deals 40 damage instead of 20.

💚 Heal

Restore 25 HP instantly. Cannot exceed 100 HP.

🔄 Swap

Swaps current bullet with next bullet in chamber.

🔮 Future Sight

Shows the next 3 bullets in order.

Strategy Tips

  • Use X-Ray before important shots to avoid wasting turns
  • Save Heal for critical moments (below 40 HP)
  • Double Damage is most effective when opponent is low on health
  • Skip Turn can secure consecutive attacks for finishing blow
  • Future Sight helps plan 2-3 turns ahead
  • Shooting yourself with blank = free turn, but risky!
